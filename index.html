About Spot.

"Everything you love about AirBnB and VRBO for finding parking spots!"

Going to that concert next to TD Garden in Boston?

How about the NY Philharmonic at Lincoln Center? Where will you park nearby? Use spot to rent a parking spot for just the hours you need it.

This app was created as part of the 2020 Hack@WPI Hackathon at Worcester Polytechnic Institute.

History of our Project:

  • January 17, 2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Four people came together, first as individuals, then as a team. We decided to pool our resources and develop a service to crowd source the parking of cars.

  • January 18, 2020

    An Application is Born!

    After a few too many Monster Energy drinks, we began working on the databases, graphic designs and maps integration that will pull together and allow the renting of parking spaces to the general public.

  • Late Evening of January 18, 2020

    Finalization of the Ideal UX

    Along with creating a functional prototype fo the website, we wanted to design the ideal user experience for the program that we would like to see. By now, we'd finalized the function of all of the interactive elements to work like the final product should.

  • January 19, 2020

    Hackathon Ends, Fun Begins

    Once we finished rushing to add finishing touches, we brought this idea to the world! Go find your parking spot now!!
